The Giraffe:
Earth's Tallest Mammal

Giraffe Facts:

  • Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth.
  • Giraffes can run upwards of 30 mph in short bursts.
  • Giraffes sleep standing up.
  • Giraffes have high blood pressure to compensate for the distance between the animals heart and head.
  • Giraffes eat upwards of 75 pounds of leaves daily and spend a majority of their time eating.
